Narration:

I remember when I first started liking him, I felt his emotional stability was like a sanctuary. He spoke so gently, and big things that would normally make me anxious would suddenly calm down just by him saying it was okay. It was magical. I thought, life with him in the future would definitely be happy. At that time, I hadn't even seen his face. But I just felt that living with him would be as soft as sun-dried cotton. He was like my tranquilizer; just being with him would make my whole being relax, feel peaceful and comfortable. But looking back now, maybe all I ever enjoyed was his gentleness. We didn't have the courage to accept the entirety of each other. He was afraid of trouble, and my inferiority complex outweighed the feelings I thought could bridge any distance. ↘ 意识到自己的“自卑”阻碍了情感的健康发展，显现出深度自我觉察。
