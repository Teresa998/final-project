We weren't actually dating then, but I presumptuously assumed he was already mine. ↘ 自我認知與現實脫節的反思，identity





I felt like I was in a relationship. The passionate happiness clouded my judgment. But then I started to feel something was off. I noticed that when I wanted to talk about things other than games, his attitude made me feel like he wasn't that interested. He gave me the feeling that he had nothing else to do besides playing games. He didn't go out or read; it seemed like his only leisure activity was gaming.
